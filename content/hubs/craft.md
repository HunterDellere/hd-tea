<p>Every difference you can taste was made somewhere: in the garden, at the pluck, on the withering rack, in the roasting basket, or in the years a cake spends breathing in a storeroom. These pages follow the leaf in that order. Cultivation covers the plant and its year. Harvest covers plucking standards and flushes. Processing covers the machinery of transformation, oxidation above all. Storage covers what time does to a finished tea, on purpose and by accident.</p>
