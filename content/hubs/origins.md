<p>A tea is a place before it is a flavor. Elevation sets the growing speed, cloud cover sets the chemistry, soil sets the finish, and the local processing tradition sets everything else. Each region entry carries a terroir map, the numbers that matter (elevation, harvest windows, signature cultivars), and links to the teas on the shelf that come from it. Regions are held to the strictest sourcing standard in the atlas: two independent sources before any entry is marked verified.</p>
